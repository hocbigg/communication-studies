# Other curricula

This curriculum is one of a few efforts to inform learners how they can pursue Communication Studies independently. Below are a few other such efforts.

- [Communication Studies Open Educational Resources (ASCCC OERI)](https://asccc-oeri.org/open-educational-resources-and-communication-studies/)
- [Survey of Communication Study (OER Commons)](https://oercommons.org/courses/survey-of-communication-study/view)
- [Open Educational Resources in Communication (University of Massachusetts Amherst)](https://guides.library.umass.edu/communication/OER)
- [Communication Open Textbooks (GALILEO Open Learning Materials)](https://oer.galileo.usg.edu/communication-textbooks/)
